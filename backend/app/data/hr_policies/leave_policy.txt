LEAVE POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
- Full-time employees: 20 days per year
- Part-time employees: Pro-rated based on hours worked
- Annual leave accrues monthly and can be carried forward up to 5 days to the next year
- Minimum booking period: 1 day
- Maximum continuous leave: 10 working days (requires manager approval for longer periods)
- Requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for planned leave

2. SICK LEAVE
- Full-time employees: 10 days per year
- Part-time employees: Pro-rated based on hours worked
- Sick leave can be taken in half-day increments
- Medical certificate required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days
- Unused sick leave does not carry forward to the next year

3. PARENTAL LEAVE
- Maternity Leave: 12 weeks paid leave
- Paternity Leave: 2 weeks paid leave
- Adoption Leave: 12 weeks paid leave
- Parental leave must be taken within 12 months of birth or adoption
- Requests should be submitted at least 4 weeks in advance

4. EMERGENCY LEAVE
- Up to 3 days per year for family emergencies
- Requires manager approval
- May require documentation depending on circumstances

5. LEAVE REQUEST PROCESS
- All leave requests must be submitted through the HR system
- Approval is typically processed within 2 business days
- Employees will receive email confirmation upon approval
- Cancellation of approved leave requires 48 hours notice

6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
- Company observes 10 public holidays per year
- Public holidays are in addition to annual leave entitlement
- If a public holiday falls on a weekend, it will be observed on the following Monday

7. LEAVE BALANCE
- Employees can check their leave balance at any time through the HR portal
- Leave balances are updated in real-time upon approval
