EMPLOYEE BENEFITS POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. HEALTH INSURANCE
- Company provides comprehensive health insurance for all full-time employees
- Coverage includes medical, dental, and vision care
- Dependents can be added at employee's expense
- Coverage begins on the first day of employment

2. RETIREMENT PLAN
- Company matches 401(k) contributions up to 5% of salary
- Employees are eligible after 90 days of employment
- Vesting period: 3 years

3. LIFE INSURANCE
- Company provides basic life insurance coverage equal to 1x annual salary
- Additional coverage available at employee's expense
- Beneficiaries can be designated through HR portal

4. DISABILITY INSURANCE
- Short-term disability: 60% of salary for up to 6 months
- Long-term disability: 60% of salary after 6 months
- Coverage begins after 30 days of employment

5. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance and certification programs covered
- Requires manager approval
- Must be related to current role or career development

6. WELLNESS PROGRAM
- Gym membership reimbursement: Up to $50 per month
- Annual health screening provided at no cost
- Mental health support: 6 free counseling sessions per year

7. TRANSPORTATION BENEFITS
- Public transportation pass reimbursement
- Parking reimbursement for office location
- Maximum reimbursement: $150 per month

8. MEAL BENEFITS
- Free lunch provided on-site Monday through Friday
- Snacks and beverages available throughout the day
- Catered meals for team meetings and special events

9. TIME OFF BENEFITS
- See Leave Policy for details on annual leave, sick leave, and parental leave
- Birthday leave: 1 additional day off on your birthday
- Volunteer time: 2 paid days per year for community service

10. OTHER BENEFITS
- Employee referral bonus: $1,000 for successful referrals
- Flexible spending account (FSA) for healthcare and dependent care
- Employee assistance program (EAP) for personal and work-related issues
