WORK FROM HOME POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. ELIGIBILITY
All full-time and part-time employees are eligible to work from home, subject to manager approval and role requirements.

2. REQUEST PROCESS
Employees must submit a work-from-home request at least 2 business days in advance. Requests should include:
- Date(s) for remote work
- Reason for request
- Confirmation that work can be completed remotely

3. FREQUENCY
- Standard employees: Up to 2 days per week
- Senior employees: Up to 3 days per week
- Special arrangements may be made for exceptional circumstances

4. REQUIREMENTS
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download speed)
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Availability during standard business hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
- Regular check-ins with manager via video call

5. EQUIPMENT
Company will provide necessary equipment including laptop, monitor, and keyboard. Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe and ergonomic workspace.

6. COMMUNICATION
Employees working from home must:
- Respond to emails and messages within 2 hours during business hours
- Attend all scheduled meetings (virtual or in-person)
- Update calendar status to reflect remote work days

7. PERFORMANCE
Performance expectations remain the same regardless of work location. Regular performance reviews will continue as scheduled.
