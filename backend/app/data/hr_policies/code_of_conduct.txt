CODE OF CONDUCT

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. GENERAL PRINCIPLES
All employees are expected to conduct themselves with integrity, respect, and professionalism at all times. This code applies to all work-related activities, including interactions with colleagues, clients, and partners.

2. RESPECTFUL WORKPLACE
- Treat all colleagues with dignity and respect
- No harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind
- Zero tolerance for offensive language, inappropriate jokes, or discriminatory comments
- Respect diversity and inclusion in all forms

3. PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOR
- Maintain professional appearance and demeanor
- Arrive on time for meetings and appointments
- Communicate clearly and respectfully
- Follow through on commitments and deadlines

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
- Protect confidential company information
- Do not share proprietary information with unauthorized parties
- Maintain client confidentiality at all times
- Follow data protection and privacy regulations

5. CONFLICTS OF INTEREST
- Disclose any potential conflicts of interest to management
- Do not engage in activities that compete with company interests
- Avoid personal relationships that could affect business decisions

6. USE OF COMPANY RESOURCES
- Company equipment and resources are for business use only
- Personal use should be minimal and not interfere with work
- Do not download unauthorized software or access inappropriate content
- Report any security concerns immediately

7. SOCIAL MEDIA
- Be mindful of social media posts that could reflect on the company
- Do not post confidential information or negative comments about the company
- Respect colleagues' privacy on social media

8. REPORTING VIOLATIONS
- Report any violations of this code to HR or management
- Reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly
- Retaliation against those who report violations is strictly prohibited

9. CONSEQUENCES
Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

10. QUESTIONS
If you have questions about this code or need guidance on a specific situation, contact HR.
